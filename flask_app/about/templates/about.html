    Who is mapculture?

    We are a group of computer science students at the University of Oregon solving the "Travelling Salesman" problem in a loud way.

    By utilizing 2 different algorithms to help create efficient routes, we have not only solved the problem, but designed the solution to be beautifully intuitive.
